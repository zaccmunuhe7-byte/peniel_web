About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Land Investment

Penie Web is a professionally managed real estate company specializing in the acquisition, subdivision, marketing, and sale of prime land and residential plots across Kenya. With a strong presence in high-growth regions including Malindi, Gilgil, Mbaruk, Lumruti, Eburu, and Gema, we deliver legally verified, high-value properties tailored for both investors and end users.

We identify emerging corridors with strong infrastructure development potential, ensuring clients benefit from capital appreciation, accessibility, and long-term security of ownership. Our commitment to transparency, due diligence, and seamless transaction processes makes us the preferred choice for land investment in Kenya.

Whether you're looking for a residential plot, agricultural shamba, or investment opportunity, Penie Web connects you with the best land deals across Kenya's most promising locations.
